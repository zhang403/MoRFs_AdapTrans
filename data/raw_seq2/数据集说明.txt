MoRFchibi 2.0 is a specialized prediction tool designed to identify the locations of Molecular Recognition Features (MoRFs) within protein sequences.
MoRFchibi 2.0 是一款专门的预测工具，旨在识别蛋白质序列中分子识别特征（MoRFs）的位置。

MoRFchibi 2.0 generates output scores using an ensemble of logistic regression convolutional neural network models followed by a reverse Bayes Rule, which normalizes for the priors in the training data. As a result, MoRFchibi 2.0 scores are individually interpretable, enabling direct comparison and integration with other scores following the same output framework, such as IPA.
MoRFchibi 2.0 通过一系列逻辑回归卷积神经网络模型生成输出分数，随后采用反贝叶斯规则，对训练数据中的先验进行归一化。因此，MoRFchibi 2.0 分数可单独解释，便于与遵循相同输出框架的其他评分（如 IPA）直接比较和整合。
